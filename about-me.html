About Me

I’m a data analyst who is drawn to the intersection of data, science, and people. With experience in government analytics and research, I specialize in transforming raw data into clear, actionable insights that guide strategy and policy. I’m skilled in R, SQL, and Power BI, and I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments that value curiosity, experimentation, and efficiency. Above all, I'm motivated by using data to bring about meaningful and measurable change.

I bring experience across government, international nonprofit organization, and academia, which has nurtured a high standard of rigor, transparency, and clear communication in all my endeavors. Much of my recent professional work cannot be shared publicly due to government data restrictions, so the projects featured here are independent explorations driven by curiosity.

Outside of work, I’m an avid skier and cook, who also enjoys watercolour painting, running, and listening to psychology podcasts. One of life’s greatest pleasures is the pursuit of novelty, and I like to allow my hobbies to evolve as new interests and challenges emerge.
